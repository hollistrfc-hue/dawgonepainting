About Us

40 Years. One Standard.

Steve and his crew have been painting homes and businesses in Stillwater and the surrounding areas for over 40 years. What started as a passion for doing the job right has grown into one of the most trusted names in local painting.

We handle everything — interior walls, exterior siding, custom texture work, drywall repair, and light carpentry. Every job gets the same attention whether it's one room or a full commercial buildout.

We're fully insured, straightforward to work with, and we don't walk away until you're happy.

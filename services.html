Our Services

We handle all aspects of paint, texture, and repair — for homes and businesses throughout Stillwater and surrounding areas.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and cabinets. We prep every surface properly so the finish lasts — no cutting corners, no shortcuts on prep work. The difference between a paint job that looks great for a year and one that lasts a decade is in how the surface gets treated before the first coat goes on.

Exterior Painting

Siding, fascia, shutters, porches. We power wash, repair, and prime before a single brush touches the surface. Oklahoma weather is hard on exterior paint — we use quality materials and do the prep work that makes a real difference when the seasons turn.

Custom Painting & Texture

Decorative finishes, faux textures, accent walls, and specialty coatings that set your space apart. If you have a vision for what you want a room to feel like, bring it to us. We've been doing custom work long enough to know how to make it happen.

Drywall Repair & Prep

Cracks, holes, water damage — we fix it before we paint it. No shortcuts. Painting over damaged drywall doesn't hide the problem, it highlights it. We take the time to get the wall right first so the finished product actually looks finished.

Light Carpentry & Trim Work

Baseboards, crown molding, door frames. The details that make a paint job look finished. We handle light carpentry so you're not juggling multiple contractors — we'll get the trim right and paint it right in the same visit.

Commercial Services

Office spaces, retail, rental properties. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption to your business. We've worked commercial jobs long enough to know that downtime costs money — we plan accordingly and show up when we say we will.
